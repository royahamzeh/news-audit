Trump says US-Iran deal to be signed on Sunday as Tehran casts doubt on timing

US President Donald Trump's recent announcement has set the political stage abuzz as he indicated that a landmark US-Iran deal is poised to be signed on the upcoming Sunday. This development comes as both nations aim to address and resolve longstanding issues pertaining to nuclear capabilities and sanctions that have led to protracted discord over the years. The anticipation surrounding this potential deal is palpable, with many international observers focused not only on the timeline but also on the substantive content that the agreement would encompass. As diplomatic channels remain engaged in what appear to be the final stages of negotiation, all eyes are now on whether the two nations can harmonize their timelines and whether this deal will indeed mark a turning point in US-Iran relations. Until an official announcement is made, conjecture and speculation continue to proliferate, highlighting the high stakes involved.
