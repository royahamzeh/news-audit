'Most massive' Russian attack on Kyiv kills at least 18

Massive Attack on Kyiv

The recent assault on Kyiv, described as the 'most massive' attack, has resulted in the tragic loss of at least 18 lives. Mayor Vitali Klitschko has declared a day of mourning as the city grapples with the aftermath of this devastating event.

Details of the Attack

  • The attack involved drones and missiles aimed at strategic targets.
  • Widespread destruction was reported across the city.
  • Residents experienced panic and chaos as buildings were damaged.
  • Emergency services worked to rescue survivors and treat the injured.

International Reaction

The international community has widely condemned the attack, calling for an end to the escalating violence in Ukraine. Leaders have emphasized the necessity of a peaceful resolution, urging all parties to engage in diplomatic discussions.

Ukrainian Response

Ukraine has appealed for greater support from its allies, stressing the need for defensive measures to protect its citizens. The attack has highlighted the urgent requirement for increased security and assistance.
