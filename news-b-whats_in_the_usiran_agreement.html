What's in the US-Iran agreement?

Key Points of the US-Iran Agreement

  • End to Fighting: The agreement sets the stage for the cessation of hostilities, marking a step towards long-term peace.
  • Nuclear Weapons: Iran commits to never developing or acquiring nuclear weapons, addressing international security concerns.
  • $300 Billion Redevelopment Package: To revitalize Iran's economy, the US and allies offer a substantial redevelopment package targeting critical infrastructure.
