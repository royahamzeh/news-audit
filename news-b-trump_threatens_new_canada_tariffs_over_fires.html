Trump threatens new Canada tariffs over fires sending 'filthy' air into US cities

Trump's Tariff Threat Over Smoke Concerns

Former President Donald Trump has issued a threat to introduce new tariffs on Canada. This threat is in response to the smoke from Canadian wildfires polluting US air, affecting cities across the northeastern United States. The poor air quality has led to health advisories.

Canada's Response and Climate Cooperation

Canadian leader Mark Carney responded by highlighting the need for cooperation between the US and Canada to tackle climate change. According to experts, climate change is escalating the conditions that lead to more frequent and severe wildfires.

  • Trump's Position: Suggests holding Canada responsible through tariffs
  • Carney's View: Stresses joint effort in combating global climate change
  • Expert Opinion: Propose collaborative policies over protectionist measures
