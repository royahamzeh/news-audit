Iranian Oil Tankers Breach US Blockade

Iran successfully maneuvered three tankers loaded with crude oil past the US military blockade in the Gulf of Oman. Despite the looming US military presence, the tankers navigated safely through the blockade line, underscoring Iran's determination to continue its oil exports.
