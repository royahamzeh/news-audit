Marine Le Pen to run for French presidency and appeal conviction in top court

Marine Le Pen Declares Candidacy for French Presidency

Marine Le Pen, the prominent leader of the hard-right National Rally, has announced her bid for the French presidency, concluding months of anticipation. This announcement comes shortly after a court ruling that permits her to run with the stipulation of wearing an electronic tag for a year. Le Pen plans to contest this conviction in France's highest court.

Key Details

  • Le Pen is set to appeal her conviction related to her candidacy.
  • An electronic tag is mandated by the court as a condition for her presidential run.
  • Le Pen's campaign is likely to focus on nationalism and immigration.
