Trump Declares Ceasefire with Iran 'Over'

Just ahead of the NATO summit, President Trump has declared the ceasefire between the United States and Iran to be 'over' due to recent mutual military strikes. The unexpected announcement has turned eyes towards Washington as political analysts anticipate its effects on both international diplomacy and security.

NATO Concerns

  • Potential strain within NATO as member states may have diverging views on the US-Iran situation.
  • Possible impact on discussions regarding collective security and Middle Eastern stability.

Open to Negotiations

Despite the declaration, Trump mentioned that US negotiators remain open to dialogue with Iran, albeit contingent on willingness from the Iranian side.
