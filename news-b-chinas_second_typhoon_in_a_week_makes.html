China Hit by Second Typhoon in a Week

The eastern region of China is grappling with the aftermath of a second typhoon making landfall, resulting in the evacuation of nearly two million people.

Evacuation Efforts

Zhejiang province took timely actions, relocating nearly two million residents as the severe weather approached. The city of Wenzhou, perilously close to the storm's path, faced imminent threats of destruction.

Response and Preparedness

  • Authorities reinforced flood defenses.
  • Rapid response teams were deployed.
  • Temporary shelters were organized for the displaced.
