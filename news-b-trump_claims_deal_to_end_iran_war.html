Trump claims deal to end Iran war near as Tehran says 'nothing' finalised

Trump Announces Prospective Deal with Iran

US President Donald Trump has announced that a "great settlement" to end the conflict with Iran is on the brink of completion. According to Trump, the agreement, if finalized, could mark a significant turnaround in US-Iran relations, which have been strained for years.

Iran's Cautious Stance

Contrasting sharply with Trump's optimism, Iranian officials have described the reports of a deal as "speculative." Tehran emphasized that ongoing discussions have yet to yield any conclusive results.

  • Iran calls reports speculative, emphasizing ongoing dialogue.
  • International community remains cautiously optimistic about potential peace.
