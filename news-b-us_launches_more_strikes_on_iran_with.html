The United States has launched airstrikes on Iranian targets, escalating the conflict between the two nations. President Donald Trump attributed these latest attacks to 'retribution for yesterday's bombing of ships by Iran' in the Persian Gulf.

  • President Trump emphasized the necessity of defending international shipping lanes.
  • Iranian state media reports casualties and damage in affected areas.
  • Iran condemns U.S. actions as violations of international law.
  • Both nations brace for potential retaliation and further conflict.
