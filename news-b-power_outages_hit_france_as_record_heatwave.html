Power outages hit France as record heatwave set to peak

Power Outages in Brittany Amid Record Heatwave

France is currently dealing with significant power outages affecting approximately 68,000 homes in the Brittany region. These outages coincide with a record-breaking heatwave, placing immense strain on the electricity grid.

Details

  • Location: Brittany, France
  • Number of Affected Homes: 68,000
  • Expected Restoration: Wednesday night
  • Issue: Strain on the grid due to high temperatures and increased use of cooling devices

Challenges and Measures

The unusually high temperatures have escalated the demand for electricity, leading to significant outages. Energy companies are currently working to restore power, but complete restoration is not anticipated until mid-week.
