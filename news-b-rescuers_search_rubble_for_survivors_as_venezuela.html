Rescuers search rubble for survivors as Venezuela earthquakes kill at least 188

Devastating Earthquakes Hit Venezuela

Venezuela experienced two catastrophic earthquakes of over seven magnitude, which struck seconds apart, resulting in significant loss of life and injury.

Current Impact

  • Confirmed fatalities: 188
  • Injured individuals: Nearly 1,500
  • Buildings destroyed and infrastructure heavily damaged

Rescue Operations

Emergency response teams are tirelessly searching through debris for survivors, facing challenges such as unstable structures and difficult-to-access areas.

International Aid

The global community has responded with offers of aid and resources to assist Venezuelan authorities and rescue workers.

Government Response

  • State of emergency declared in affected regions
  • Deployment of military support to maintain order
  • Prioritizing relief efforts for the impacted population
