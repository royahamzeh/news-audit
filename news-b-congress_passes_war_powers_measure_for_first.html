Congress Passes Historic War Powers Measure

In a decisive move, Congress has passed its first war powers measure aimed at limiting the President’s military actions against Iran, sending a clear message of repudiation to former President Donald Trump’s military strategies.

Symbolic Yet Significant

  • The resolution is largely symbolic.
  • It reaffirms Congressional authority over war powers.
  • It aims to increase pressure on the White House to end military engagements.

Calls for Diplomacy

Supporters emphasize the need for diplomatic solutions and express concerns about the sustainability of prolonged military conflicts.
