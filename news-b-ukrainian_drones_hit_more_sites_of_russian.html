Ukrainian Drone Strikes on Wildberries

  • Two regions targeted: Krasnodar and Stavropol.

The ongoing conflict between Ukraine and Russia has taken a new turn with Ukrainian drones targeting logistics hubs belonging to Russia's largest online retailer, Wildberries. Located in the strategic regions of Krasnodar and Stavropol, these hubs are crucial for maintaining the retailer's vast distribution network across the country. As tensions escalate, such attacks imply a strategic focus by Ukraine on disrupting economic infrastructure, potentially leading to increased pressure on Russia through delays in supply chain operations. This move marks a notable shift in the approach, highlighting how modern conflict increasingly intertwines with the economic sphere, affecting not only military targets but also commercial entities that are key to a nation's economy.
