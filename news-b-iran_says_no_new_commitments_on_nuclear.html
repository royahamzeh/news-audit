Iran's Position on Nuclear Inspections

In the wake of recent discussions in Switzerland, Iran has stated that it has made "no new commitments" regarding nuclear inspections. This comes after statements by Vance suggesting inspectors would be invited back, but Iran's foreign ministry has clarified their position.

Talks in Switzerland
