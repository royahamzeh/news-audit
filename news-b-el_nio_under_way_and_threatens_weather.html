El Niño under way and threatens weather extremes, scientists say

El Niño and Potential Weather Extremes

According to US scientists, an El Niño event has officially commenced, signaling possible extreme weather conditions globally.

Key Points

  • El Niño involves the warming of ocean surface temperatures in the eastern Pacific.
  • Potential impacts include increased temperatures, storms, droughts, and weather disruptions worldwide.
  • Scientists are using predictive models to understand and anticipate these changes.

Preparation and Mitigation

Authorities are advised to prepare for potential weather extremes such as intensified rainfall, flooding, and severe droughts. Planning and mitigation efforts are crucial to reduce the risks and economic impacts.

Summary
The commencement of the El Niño event raises concerns about global weather extremities, with scientists urging preparedness and continuous monitoring.
