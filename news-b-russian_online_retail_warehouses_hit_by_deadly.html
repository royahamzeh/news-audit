Ukrainian Drones Strike Russian Warehouses

In a critical event, Ukrainian drones have struck Wildberries warehouses in Russia, with sites affected near Moscow and in Tambov.

Key Details

  • Location: Moscow and Tambov
  • Target: Wildberries warehouses
  • Impact: Casualties reported; disruptions in logistics

Statements and Reactions

Ukraine's President Volodymyr Zelenskyy characterized the facilities as 'major logistics centers' violating sanctions by distributing 'sanctioned components.' This act forms part of broader tactical maneuvers to weaken components of Russia’s logistical chains, highlighting a severe area of vulnerability within this ongoing conflict, amid already existing tensions sparked by international sanctions.
