Ukrainian suspect hunted by police after Monaco bomb attack was 'disguised as a man'

In an unsettling development, authorities in Monaco are actively pursuing a Ukrainian suspect believed to have orchestrated a recent bomb attack. The suspect has reportedly been disguising herself as a man, complicating efforts to apprehend her. Intelligence suggests that the perpetrator spent several days surveying the target location, meticulously planning the assault. This revelation underscores concerns within the investigative community that the suspect did not act in isolation. Instead, it's suspected that she was part of a larger network, potentially with accomplices who supported her efforts either directly or indirectly. The attack has sent shockwaves through Monaco, a region unaccustomed to such acts of violence. Authorities are working around the clock to dismantle any networks that may be involved. Amidst heightened security alerts, residents and tourists alike are urged to maintain vigilance as the hunt intensifies. Forensic teams are meticulously examining the explosion site for additional clues, while law enforcement agencies collaborate internationally to track down the suspect and any associates. This incident has prompted an urgent review of security protocols, aiming to prevent similar episodes in the future. As the investigation unfolds, officials remain tight-lipped about specific details, emphasizing the critical nature of confidentiality to avoid jeopardizing ongoing operations.
