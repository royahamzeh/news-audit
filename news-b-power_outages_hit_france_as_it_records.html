Power outages hit France as it records hottest day since measurements began

Historic Heat Wave in France

  • France experiences the hottest day since 1947.
  • New temperature record breaks that of Tuesday.
  • Power outages linked to increased electricity demand.

Impact of Extreme Weather

  • Blackouts affect several regions.
  • Increased use of cooling systems strains power infrastructure.
  • Authorities advise limitations on electrical appliance use.

Government and Public Response

  • Efforts to restore electricity ongoing.
  • Support focuses on vulnerable populations.
  • Citizens urged to stay hydrated and indoors.
