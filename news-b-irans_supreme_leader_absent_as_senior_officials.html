Iran's Supreme Leader Absent from Ayatollah's Funeral

In an unexpected twist, Iran's supreme leader Ali Khamenei was absent from the funeral of Ayatollah Ebrahim Amini, raising questions and speculation.

Key Points:

  • The funeral was held on Sunday and attended by several senior officials.
  • Khamenei's son, Mojtaba, was also missing from the event.
  • Speculation is rampant about Mojtaba Khamenei's condition and health.
