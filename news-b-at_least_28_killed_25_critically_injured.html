At least 28 killed, 25 critically injured after fire engulfs Bangkok bar

Tragic Fire at Bangkok Bar

A devastating fire broke out at a bustling bar in Bangkok’s Chatuchak district, leading to a tragic death toll of at least 28 people and critically injuring 25 others. The horrific incident unfolded late in the evening, engulfing the popular entertainment venue in flames.

Firefighting Efforts

  • Firefighters were confronted with chaotic scenes of patrons fleeing through flames.
  • Thick smoke and intense heat complicated rescue efforts.
  • Emergency responders worked tirelessly to control the blaze and evacuate remaining patrons.

Eyewitness Accounts

Eyewitnesses reported cries for help and difficulties in escaping due to blocked exits and dense crowds.

Investigation and Concerns

Authorities have begun investigating the cause of the fire, with questions surrounding safety standards and compliance with fire codes.

Community Impact

This tragedy has left families and friends of the victims grappling with the sudden and violent disruption of their lives. The incident highlights the urgent need for improved safety measures in entertainment venues.
